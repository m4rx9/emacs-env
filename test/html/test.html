Would you like to take part in an inspiring event?

Join our Opening of the Academic Year 2013/2014 at Biocentrum Ochota, Warsaw!

Friday 25.10.2013





Are you a PhD student? Register now!

Our Scientific Committee based on submitted abstracts will select 30 PhD students for a poster session with an award for the best poster.

You are not a PhD student of Biocentrum Ochota. No problem! The competition is open for all PhD students in Life sciences from Poland, Europe and all around the world!
Do Science and join us!

Authors of three the best abstracts will give short talks and the best speaker will win 300€ 500€!!!

registration deadline 01/10/2013 extended: 04/10/2013

Poster: A0, portrait orientation. Notification of abstract acceptance/rejection: Friday, 11/10/2013 (one week after the registration deadline). It very much depends on the number of submissions. It gives you two weeks to prepare your talk or poster in a case your abstract has been accepted.

Keynote speakers
prof. Gottfried Schatz

Professor emeritus, Biozentrum, University of Basel, Switzerland

Prof. Schatz has made pioneering and seminal discoveries in the field of mitochondrial DNA and protein biogenesis.
He has got interests in science policy and is an author of the books for general audience about the science and scientists, for example JEFF'S VIEW on Science and Scientists.

prof. Ineke Braakman

Utrecht University, the Netherlands

Prof. Braakman is a leading scientist in the field of protein folding. Her major research interests are protein folding and modification processes in the endoplasmic reticulum of mammalian cells and their links to diseases.

EMBO is supporting a lecture of prof. Schatz Dear EMBO, thank you very much!

Sponsors of the awards are: VitaInSilica, Life Technologies, Eppendorf, Sigma-Aldrich.
Thank you very much & welcome on board!
